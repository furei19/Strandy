What is the Sports Track?

The Sports track is a specialized educational program designed for students with a keen interest and talent in sports and physical activities. It offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines academic subjects with sports training and development.

Track Overview

The Sports track focuses on physical education, sports science, specialized training, and competitions. Students engage in various sports, develop athletic skills, and learn about sports-related topics like nutrition and psychology. They receive specialized training, enhance their physical fitness, and participate in competitions. The track prepares students for careers in sports and related fields or higher education in sports disciplines. It offers a unique educational experience combining academics with sports training.
