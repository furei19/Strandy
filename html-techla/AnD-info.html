What is the Arts and Design strand?

The Arts and Design Strand provides students with a platform to explore their artistic talents, develop creative thinking, and gain a deep appreciation for various art forms. It encourages self-expression, fosters critical analysis, and nurtures the skills necessary for pursuing further education or careers in the arts and design industries.

Strand Overview

The Arts and Design Strand in high school offers a wide range of subjects that nurture students' artistic and creative abilities. It includes visual arts, performing arts, design, creative writing, art appreciation, and portfolio development. Students explore various art forms, develop their skills in music, dance, theater, and film, learn about design principles, engage in creative writing, appreciate and critique art, and build portfolios to showcase their work. This strand provides students with a comprehensive arts education experience, preparing them for further studies or careers in the arts and related fields.
