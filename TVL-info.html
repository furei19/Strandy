TVL Strand

What is the TVL strand?

The TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood) strand is an educational program or track offered in some high schools that focuses on providing students with technical and vocational skills relevant to various industries and livelihood opportunities. It aims to equip students with practical skills and knowledge that can lead to immediate employment or further studies in specific vocational fields.

Strand Overview

The TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood) strand offers students specialized vocational subjects, work immersion opportunities, entrepreneurship training, and general education subjects. It focuses on developing practical skills, providing real-world experiences, and preparing students for immediate employment or further education. Students can earn industry-recognized certifications, enhancing their employability in specific vocational fields. The TVL strand provides a pathway to both career readiness and higher education in technical or vocational institutions.
