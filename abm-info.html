What is the ABM strand?

The ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) strand is an educational program or track offered in some high schools that focuses on subjects related to finance, entrepreneurship, and business management. It aims to provide students with a strong foundation in business principles and prepare them for careers in the fields of accounting, finance, marketing, human resources, and general business management.

Strand Overview

It covers subjects like financial accounting, business management, economics, entrepreneurship, and mathematics. Students learn about financial statements, marketing, market dynamics, starting businesses, and practical applications. The ABM strand integrates these subjects to provide a holistic understanding of the business environment and emphasizes practical skills through case studies, simulations, and internships. Its goal is to equip students for future careers and further education in business-related fields.
