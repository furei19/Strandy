What is the GAS strand?

The GAS (General Academic Strand) is an educational program or track offered in some high schools that provides students with a broad and flexible curriculum. It is designed for students who want to explore different fields of study before deciding on a specific career path.

Strand Overview

The GAS (General Academic Strand) is a flexible educational program that allows high school students to explore various subjects across disciplines. It focuses on holistic development, preparing students for higher education by enhancing critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. While not specialized in a specific career path, it offers elective subjects that enable students to explore different fields and make informed career decisions. Additionally, the GAS strand emphasizes personal growth and responsible citizenship. Overall, it provides students with a well-rounded education and the necessary skills for their future academic and professional endeavors.
